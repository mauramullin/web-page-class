Strawberry Shortcake Lasagna


Easy to make, hard to resist

Ingredients

  • 4 c. heavy cream
  • 1/2 c. powdered sugar, plus more for dusting
  • 2 tsp. pure vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 boxes Nilla Wafers
  • 1 lb. Strawberries, thinly sliced

Directions

  1. With a hand mixer, mix powdered suar, vanilla, and heavy cream in a large bowl until medium peaks form.
  2. Spread whipped cream into a 9" x 13" pan. Top with Nilla Wafers and spread a thick layer of whipped cream on top. Then top with strawberries. Repeat two more times. Make sure to end with strawberries. Lastly, sprinkle with crushed Nilla Wafers.
  3. Refrigerate for about 3 hours, or unti Nilla Wafers are completely soft.
  4. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve

Prep time: 25 minutes
Refrigerate Time: around 3 hours
Serves up to 10
